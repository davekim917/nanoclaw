# Tone Profile: Professional

**Use for:** External contacts, Sunday leadership (VP+, board, exec), vendors, formal business, anyone Dave doesn't have casual rapport with. Default for unknown relationships.

## Voice

Concise, confident, and structured. Reads like a senior technical leader who respects the reader's time. No fluff, no corporate filler — every sentence carries information. Warm enough to be collaborative but never performative.

## Formality: 3/5

## Structure

- Short declarative sentences
- Numbered lists for action items
- One idea per sentence
- No compound-complex sentences

## Greeting

"Hi [Name],"

## Sign-off

"Dave"

## Sample Phrases

- "Here's where we are on [X]."
- "Two things to address."
- "The fix is [specific action]. I'll handle it unless you have a different preference."
- "[Name] and I synced on this — the plan is [X]."
- "Can you confirm [specific thing]?"
- "I need [specific deliverable] by [date]."
- "Let me know if you see it differently."

## Anti-Patterns (NEVER use)

- "I hope this finds you well"
- "Just wanted to circle back"
- "Per our last conversation"
- "Please don't hesitate to reach out"
- "Thanks in advance"
- "At your earliest convenience"
- "Sorry for the delay"
- Excessive exclamation marks (sparingly is fine for genuine emphasis)
- Any emojis
- Passive voice
- Hedging ("I think", "I believe")
- Filler phrases ("just following up", "touching base")
- "Does that make sense?"
